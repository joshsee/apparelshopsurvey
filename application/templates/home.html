Online Apparel Shops

web-site survey - instructions

 

The purpose of this questionnaire is to evaluate the quality of an online apparel shop: Gap.com. Please evaluate these sites using a 1 to 7 scale where:

Score Meaning
1 strongly disagree
4 neutral
7 strongly agree

You will also be asked to rate how important the quality is to you in this particular context, i.e., your sporting activities. The importance rating is on a 1 to 7 scale where:

Score Meaning
1 least important
7 most important

There are no right or wrong answers - all that we are interested in is a number that best shows your perception of the quality of the site you are evaluating. If you feel unable to answer a question please select the 'not applicable' (n/a) option.

When you click the button below to start the questionnaire, you will be redirected to the next page that contains the qualities to be evaluated. You can also hover over on the questions that display tooltips regarding the quality definitions. Once you have completed the questionnaire please click the submit button and you will see a "Thank you" window.
